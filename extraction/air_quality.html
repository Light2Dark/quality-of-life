Legend / Information

  • **
    Particulate matter with diameter of less than 2.5 micron (PM2.5) / μg/m3
    *
    Particulate matter with diameter of less than 10 micron (PM10) / μg/m3
    a
    Sulphur Dioxide (SO2) / ppm
    b
    Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2) / ppm
    c
    Ozone (O3) / ppm
    d
    Carbon Monoxide (CO) / ppm
    &
    More than one pollutant

    Note: API is calculated based on 24hr running average of the most dominant parameter.

    A specific colour shape is assigned to each Air Pollutant Index (API) category to make it easier for the people with colour vision deficiency to understand the status of air quality. The shapes are shown as below:

    Good
    0 - 50
    Moderate
    51 - 100
    Unhealthy
    101 - 200
    Very Unhealthy
    201 - 300
    Hazardous
    Above 300
